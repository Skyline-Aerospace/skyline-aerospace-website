About Us

Welcome to Skyline Aerospace, a club at Vandegrift High School that specializes in the research and development of advanced next generation concepts in the field of aerospace technology. Our mission is to launch tethered high altitude weather balloons to explore the possibilities of space travel and other applications of aerospace technology. We are passionate about pushing the boundaries of what is possible and are constantly seeking new and innovative ways to improve upon existing technologies. As members of Skyline Aerospace, you will have the opportunity to work on cutting-edge projects and collaborate with like-minded students who are eager to make a difference in the field of aerospace. We invite you to join us on our journey to the stars and beyond!
